№ 02 / Floor plan

The forge floor.
Six stations, one bench.

Most AI tools are built like vending machines: prompt in, answer out. PrivateForge is a workshop. Each station does one thing well. They share a bench, your memory, and the same set of tools — so a research note can become a draft, a draft can become a build, a build can become a scheduled task. Without leaving your laptop.

№ 03 / Specifications

The spec sheet.
What you actually get.

We do not sell magic. PrivateForge is a small piece of software that runs on your machine, talks to the AI providers you already pay for, and lets you do work that you would otherwise do in eight different tabs.

Where it runs Your machine — macOS or Windows. Self-contained app, no cloud sandbox required.
Who sees your data Only you and the model provider you choose. PrivateForge is not in the request path.
Models Bring your own keys — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, plus open-source via local inference. Switch per task.
Cost One flat license, paid yearly. Token spend goes to your provider, not to us.
Connectors Gmail · Calendar · Drive · GitHub · Notion · Linear · Stripe · Slack · + a growing set
Memory Durable, searchable, editable. Stored locally; you control it; export anytime.
Audit log Every tool call, every file written, every site visited — recorded and replayable.
Open source posture Closed core, open extension surface. Bring your own tools, your own connectors.
