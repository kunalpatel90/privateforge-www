№ 02 / How the math works

What you pay,
and to whom.

Pricing on a forge has two sides. The bench (PrivateForge) costs a flat amount once a year. The materials (model tokens) you buy direct from your provider, at their list price, with no markup from us. Most teams find this is the cheapest way to run AI work — because we are not in the request path, there is no margin for us to take.

License fee $120 / yr solo · $96 / yr / seat for teams of 5+. Paid to PrivateForge.
Token spend Whatever your model provider charges. Paid directly to Anthropic / OpenAI / Google. We never touch it.
Local models Free at runtime. llama.cpp / MLX / Ollama. Your hardware, your cost.
Connectors Included. Auth tokens stored locally. We do not see them.
Storage Local. Memory and audit log live on your disk. No cloud sync unless you turn it on.
Cloud sync (optional) Included. End-to-end encrypted. Off by default.
№ 03 / Frequently asked

The questions
everyone asks.

Is there a free version?
There is a 14-day free trial on Solo and Team. No card needed. Local models work without any provider keys, so you can drive the bench without spending a dollar.
Do I need an Anthropic / OpenAI / Google account?
Only if you want to use those providers. PrivateForge ships with local model support out of the box. Add cloud keys when you want them.
Can I cancel and keep my work?
Yes. The license is annual; if you stop renewing, the app keeps running on the last installed version and your local files stay where they are. There is nothing to "export" because the work was never on our servers.
Is the token spend really at-cost?
Yes. Your provider key, your account, your invoice. We are not a reseller and we do not see the bill.
Do you offer student or non-profit pricing?
Yes. Email hello@privateforge.ai with proof of status.
What happens after the trial?
Cloud-backed features pause until you pick a plan. Local models, local files, and local memory keep working.
